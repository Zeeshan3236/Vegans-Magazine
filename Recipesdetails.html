Sausage with Peppers and Onions (8)

  • Average Cooking Time: 35 mins

This is one of my go to mid-week dinner recipes. It requires little effort, and the result is always delicious. This Italian inspired sausage recipe is always satisfying. I like it to serve it with rice. This recipe serves 2-3 individuals.
